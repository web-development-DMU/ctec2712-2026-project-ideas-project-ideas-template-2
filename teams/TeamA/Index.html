Our application

We are building the Car Service Tracker, a web application for logging and managing vehicle maintenance records.

The application allows car owners to register their vehicles and keep a full log of every service, repair or MOT. An admin section allows management of service types. The goal is a clean, easy-to-use interface backed by a SQLite database and a Node.js Express server.
