Car Wash Booking System and Project Layout

This car wash booking system is proposed by Milan Paudel.

Users can create an account or log in to their existing account.

Users can search for a car wash and book an available timeslot.

Admins can create, cancel, and delete timeslots.

Car washes will be listed, along with their contact info and location.

Timeslots will also be listed for car washes and can be booked.

Front-End

Login Page: user's name, email, password, phone number, and payment methods â€“ along with the option to choose a default payment method.

Home Page: list of 5 car washes per page, filtered by location where the nearest one shows at the top, along with a photo of the car wash and a Google Maps link.

Navbar: home page button at the top left, user profile dropdown (options such as edit profile, view bookings, sign out) at the top right.

Car Wash View Page: extra information displayed for car wash (services, service fees, etc).

Make Booking Form: user prompted to select a timeslot and services at the specific car wash, toggling the available column in the timeslots table to false.

Cancel Booking Form: user prompted to cancel specific booking, toggling the available column in the timeslots table to true.

Back-End

Users Table: user_id, name, is_admin, email, password, phone number, payment_methods, default_payment_method.

Carwashes Table: carwash_id, email, phone number, postcode, address, photo.

Services Table: service_id, name, description, carwash_id, fee.

Timeslots Table: timeslot_id, start_at, end_at, available.

Bookings Table: booking_id, user_id, carwash_id, service_id, start_at, end_at, total_fee.
