Car Service Tracker

Description

Car Service Tracker is a web application that allows car owners and small garages to record and manage vehicle maintenance history. Users can add their cars, log service events such as oil changes, MOTs and tyre replacements, and view the full service history for each vehicle.

The application is designed to be simple and practical. It solves the common problem of losing track of when a car was last serviced and what work was done.

Users

  • Car owners — can add their vehicles and log service records for each one.
  • Administrators — can manage service types (e.g. oil change, MOT, full service) and oversee all records.

User stories

  • As a car owner, I want to add my car so I can track its service history.
  • As a car owner, I want to log a service record so I know what work has been done.
  • As a car owner, I want to view all past services for my car in one place.
  • As a car owner, I want to edit or delete a service record if I made a mistake.
  • As an admin, I want to manage the list of service types available.

Database structure

The application will use four tables:

  • owners — id, name, email, phone
  • cars — id, owner_id, make, model, year, reg_number
  • service_records — id, car_id, service_date, service_type_id, mileage, cost, notes
  • service_types — id, name, description

Biggest challenges

  • Linking service records correctly to the right car using foreign keys.
  • Making sure the admin and user views are clearly separated.
  • Validating form inputs server-side before writing to the database.
