Who am I?

I'm Elisha Muganda, a Computer Science student who has some with web development. I'm eager to grow my skills and take on new challenges throughout this course.

What experience do I have in web development?

My experience is fairly limited at the moment. I have a basic understanding of HTML and CSS, but would like to fully understand both. I'm still getting comfortable with how web pages are structured and styled.

What am I keen to learn from this module?

I want to properly understand how CSS works and how to use it effectively to style pages. Beyond that, I'm keen to learn how full web applications are built and how the front-end and back-end connect together.

What kind of project would I like to be involved in?

I'd like to work on something practical that I can actually see being used in real life. I want a project that challenges me to learn as I build, rather than one that relies on skills I don't yet have.

Project Ideas

  • Student Timetable Organiser - An app where students can input their modules and deadlines to keep track of their schedule in one place.
  • Skill-Exchange Platform - A place where people can offer to teach something they know and learn something they don't.
  • Campus Event Board - A site where universities can post upcoming events and students can browse what's happening when.
  • Study Group Finder - An app that enables students to find or create study groups for specific modules at their university.
  • Second Hand Textbook Marketplace - An app that allows students to buy and sell textbooks that can help with studies.
