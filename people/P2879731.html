I am Muhammad Ismail, a Computer Science student at De Montfort University with a strong interest in web development and building practical applications. I enjoy creating clean, user friendly interfaces and working on projects that solve real problems. I am interested in how simple design choices can improve user experience.

In this module, I am looking forward to improving my web development skills further, especially in building interactive features and working collaboratively in a team. My goal is to create applications that are simple, meaningful, and genuinely useful for everyday users.
